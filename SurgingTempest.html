Cosmic Clash: Surging Tempest

Unleash the storm with the Surging Tempest deck, featuring the powerful Storm Dragon and an exclusive Snowflake card with unique artwork. This starter set includes everything you need to jump into the action:

  • A pre-built Surging Tempest deck ready to play.
  • 20 black card sleeves for your Smashers and 20 red card sleeves for your assets.
  • 1 Cosmic Clash pack (9 random cards) to expand your collection.
  • A Damage Counter card to track your battles.
