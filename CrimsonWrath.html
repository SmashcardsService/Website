Elemental Fury: Crimson Wrath

Take command of the battlefield with the Crimson Wrath deck, featuring the mighty Ultra Crimson Warrior and an exclusive Magic Orb card with unique artwork. This starter set includes everything you need to jump into the action:

  • A pre-built Crimson Wrath deck ready to play.
  • 20 black card sleeves for your Smashers and 20 red card sleeves for your assets.
  • 1 Elemental Fury pack (9 random cards) to expand your collection.
  • A Damage Counter card to track your battles.

Get ready to dominate the battlefield with Crimson Wrath!
